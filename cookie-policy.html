What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device by websites that you visit. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the owners of the site.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our website, including:

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in your browser settings. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
